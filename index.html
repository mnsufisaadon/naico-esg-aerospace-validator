Technical Methodology & Audit Logic

Visible logic helps judges understand and audit the mathematical approach.

Total Net Emissions
E_net = (S₂ + S₁gse + S₁flight + S₁h2) − R_solar
Scope 2 Electricity
S₂ = Electricity(MWh) × 0.6 tCO₂e/MWh
Ground Support Equipment
S₁gse = Biodiesel(t) × 2.52 tCO₂e/t
Flight Fuel with SAF Reduction
S₁flight = JetA1(t) × 3.15 × (1 − (SAF% × 0.8))
Solar Offset
R_solar = (kWp × 1500 / 1000) × 0.6 tCO₂e
